About Collabify

Our Mission

At Collabify, our mission is to empower teams and individuals to collaborate seamlessly and creatively, no matter where they are. We believe that great ideas flourish when people can connect, share, and build together in an intuitive and engaging environment.

What We Offer

Collabify provides a dynamic online whiteboard platform designed for real-time collaboration. Our features include:

  • Real-time Drawing: Sketch, draw, and annotate with multiple users simultaneously.
  • Text and Shapes: Add text boxes, sticky notes, and various shapes to organize your thoughts.
  • Image and Document Upload: Integrate external content directly onto your whiteboard.
  • Version History: Track changes and revert to previous states of your projects.
  • Secure Sharing: Easily share your whiteboards with team members and control access permissions.
  • Intuitive Interface: A clean and user-friendly design that makes collaboration effortless.

Our Story

Collabify was founded in 2025 by a team of passionate developers and designers who saw the need for a more interactive and accessible collaboration tool. Frustrated with the limitations of existing solutions, we set out to create a platform that truly fosters creativity and teamwork. Since then, we've been continuously evolving, driven by user feedback and the ever-changing landscape of remote work.
